Common Questions

Restoration FAQ.

Is my roof a good candidate for restoration?

Most shingle roofs between 8 and 25 years old are great candidates. We do a free on-site inspection to make sure restoration is the right call — if it isn't, we'll tell you straight up.

How long does the work take?

Most homes are wrapped in a single day. Larger or steeper roofs may take two.

What's covered by the 8-year warranty?

Our written warranty covers our restoration workmanship and the protective coating. We'll come back and make it right if anything fails.

How much does it cost?

Restoration typically runs 50-70% less than a full replacement. Every estimate is free and tailored to your roof.
